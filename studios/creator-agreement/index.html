StudentStream Studios Creator Agreement

This Creator Agreement (“Agreement”) applies to all creators, artists, designers, filmmakers, and contributors (“Creator”) who submit work to StudentStream Studios (“Studios”), a division of StudentStream Productions.

1. Eligibility & Original Work

By submitting work to Studios, you confirm that:

  • You are the original creator and rights holder of all submitted content
  • Your work does not infringe on third-party rights
  • You have full authority to license and sell the work

2. Ownership & Rights

Creators retain full ownership of their original work. By submitting content, you grant StudentStream Studios a non-exclusive, worldwide license to:

  • Display, promote, and distribute the work
  • Sell or license the work through Studios
  • Use the work for marketing and promotional purposes

This license remains active while the work is listed on Studios.

3. Revenue Split & Payments

Studios operates on a creator-first revenue model:

  • 70% of net revenue is paid to the Creator
  • 30% is retained by StudentStream Studios

All payouts are processed manually via Cash App using the username provided during submission.

Creators are responsible for providing accurate payout information and handling any applicable taxes.

4. Review & Approval

All submissions are reviewed manually. StudentStream Studios reserves the right to:

  • Approve or reject submissions at its discretion
  • Remove content that violates guidelines or quality standards
  • Suspend or terminate creator participation if necessary

5. Brand Commissions & Custom Work

For commissioned or custom projects:

  • Project scope, pricing, and deadlines are agreed upon separately
  • Studios may act as an intermediary between brands and creators
  • Revenue splits may vary based on project terms

6. Termination

Creators may request removal of their work at any time. StudentStream Studios may terminate participation if this Agreement is violated. Previously completed sales are not affected.

7. Limitation of Liability

StudentStream Studios is not liable for indirect damages, lost profits, or disputes arising between creators and third parties. Participation is at your own risk.

8. Updates to Agreement

This Agreement may be updated as Studios evolves. Creators will be notified of material changes. Continued participation constitutes acceptance of updated terms.

Important: By submitting work to StudentStream Studios, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Creator Agreement.
