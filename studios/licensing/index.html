Commercial Licensing

Licensing at StudentStream Studios

StudentStream Studios provides clear, commercial-ready licenses so brands, agencies, and studios can confidently use creative assets and commissioned work.

What you CAN do

  • Use assets in paid ads, social campaigns, films, websites, and apps
  • Use content commercially for brands or clients
  • Monetize content using licensed assets
  • Commission creators for custom work

What you CANNOT do

  • Resell or redistribute assets as standalone products
  • Claim ownership of creator work
  • Use assets in illegal, hateful, or deceptive content
  • Mint assets as NFTs unless explicitly permitted

Exclusive vs Non-Exclusive

Some assets and commissions may offer exclusive licensing. Unless stated otherwise, licenses are non-exclusive.
