How StudentStream Law Works

Every report submitted through StudentStream Law follows a structured enforcement process designed for fairness, transparency, and creator protection.

1. Report is reviewed by verified enforcement staff.
2. Evidence is collected and securely documented.
3. Policy or legal evaluation is performed.
4. Action is taken: warning, restriction, takedown, or escalation.
5. Transparency logs may be updated when appropriate.
