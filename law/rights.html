Creator Rights

StudentStream is built on creator ownership. Every creator retains control over their original work, identity, and distribution.

StudentStream Law exists to enforce these protections fairly, quickly, and transparently.

Full Content Ownership You always retain full rights to the work you create and upload.
Distribution Control You control where and how your content is shared across StudentStream.
Monetization Rights Eligible creators may monetize their work without theft or unauthorized resale.
Takedown & Enforcement You may file copyright and abuse reports for stolen or reposted content.
Protection From Impersonation StudentStream Law investigates identity abuse and fake creator accounts.
Fair Review Process All enforcement actions follow structured investigation and documentation.
Need Help?
If your work has been stolen, misused, or you are being impersonated, submit an official report through the StudentStream Law portal.
