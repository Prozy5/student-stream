Founder Story

Deondre founded StudentStream with a simple idea — creators should control their platforms, their content, and their revenue.

What began as a streaming platform evolved into a full technology ecosystem built to support independent media, digital businesses, and investigative journalism.

Rather than relying on existing corporations, Deondre chose to build the infrastructure himself — connecting entertainment, law, production, and intelligence under one company.

Phase 1 — SS Films

Launch of the independent film & streaming platform.

Phase 2 — StudentStream Studios

Expansion into creator tools, production systems, and digital infrastructure.

Phase 3 — SSI Law

Creation of legal systems for creator protection and digital ownership.

Phase 4 — SS:I

Launch of investigative media focusing on hidden systems and untold narratives.

Vision

Deondre’s long-term vision is to transform StudentStream into a global infrastructure company powering independent media across multiple industries.

His goal is not only to build platforms — but to reshape who owns digital systems, information, and creative economies.

“The future belongs to creators who own their systems.”
