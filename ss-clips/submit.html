SS Clips helps creators get discovered without taking ownership of their content. You keep your video. We amplify it.

How Submissions Work

  • You submit a public video link (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, etc.)
  • Our team reviews it for quality & rule compliance
  • If accepted, we feature and promote the clip
  • You keep full ownership and monetization
