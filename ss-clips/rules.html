Submission Guidelines

SS Clips Rules

These rules exist to protect creators, viewers, and the integrity of SS Clips. Submitting content means you agree to follow these guidelines.

What You Can Submit

  • Original clips you own or have rights to
  • Publicly hosted videos (YouTube, TikTok, Twitch)
  • Creative, educational, or entertaining content
  • Clips that represent you or your brand accurately

Hard Rules (Automatic Rejection)

  • Impersonation or stolen content
  • Reuploads without permission
  • Scams, fraud, or misleading claims
  • Hate speech, harassment, or threats
  • Sexual exploitation or graphic violence
  • Malware, phishing, or unsafe links

Soft Rules (May Be Rejected)

  • Low-effort reposts
  • Clickbait titles or deceptive thumbnails
  • Excessive self-promotion without value
  • Content designed purely to farm engagement

Enforcement & Removal

SS Clips enforces rules consistently and proportionally. Actions may include rejection, removal, or account restrictions.

Enforcement decisions are independent of monetization or promotion.

Appeals

If you believe your content was removed or rejected incorrectly, you may request a review through StudentStream Security.

Appeals are reviewed by humans, not automated systems.

Why Rules Matter

Rules are not restrictions — they are protection. SS Clips exists to surface real creators, not exploit them. Clear rules ensure fairness, safety, and long-term trust.
