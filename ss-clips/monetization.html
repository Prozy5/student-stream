Creator-First Revenue Model

How SS Clips Makes Money

SS Clips was built to prove that discovery does not have to be exploitative. Monetization exists to sustain operations — not to sell attention or manipulate reach.

What Will Always Be Free

  • Submitting clips for review
  • Being featured on SS Clips
  • Basic discovery and exposure
  • Security enforcement and protection

Discovery is never pay-to-play.

What SS Clips Will Never Sell

  • ❌ Guaranteed placement
  • ❌ Algorithmic boosting
  • ❌ Suppression of other creators
  • ❌ Enforcement immunity

Planned Optional Monetization

Any monetization introduced by SS Clips will be optional, clearly labeled, and never required for participation.

  • Creator Profiles+ — enhanced public profiles, analytics, and branding
  • Submission Insights — detailed feedback and review notes
  • Brand Matchmaking — opt-in brand discovery and outreach tools
  • Enterprise Licensing — clip discovery tools for media partners

Advertising Policy

If advertising exists on SS Clips, it will:

  • Be clearly labeled
  • Never interrupt clips
  • Never influence clip selection
  • Never collect personal data without consent

Why This Model Works

SS Clips earns trust first, revenue second. By separating discovery from monetization, the platform remains fair — and sustainable.

Creators grow because their work deserves attention, not because they paid for it.
