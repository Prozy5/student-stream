How SS Clips Works

SS Clips is not algorithmic, automated, or pay-to-play. This page explains exactly how content is selected, reviewed, and protected — so creators know where they stand.

How Clips Are Selected

Every clip submitted to SS Clips is reviewed by a human. We evaluate submissions based on originality, clarity, creator intent, and community safety — not follower count or engagement farming.

  • Original content or rightful ownership
  • Clear creative or informational value
  • No deceptive edits or manipulation
  • Respect for creators, communities, and brands

What SS Clips Does NOT Do

  • ❌ No shadow banning
  • ❌ No algorithmic suppression
  • ❌ No forced trends or engagement traps
  • ❌ No selling visibility behind the scenes

If a clip is rejected, it is not punished — it is simply not featured.

Ownership & Rights

Creators retain 100% ownership of their content. SS Clips does not claim rights, relicense content, or monetize clips without creator consent.

Embedded clips remain hosted on the creator’s original platform.

Safety & Enforcement

SS Clips is protected by StudentStream Security. We actively enforce policies against impersonation, harassment, scams, and coordinated abuse.

  • Identity & impersonation review
  • Abuse and fraud reporting
  • Evidence-based enforcement
  • Appeal and audit pathways

Monetization Transparency

SS Clips does not charge creators to be discovered. Any future monetization will be:

  • Optional
  • Clearly labeled
  • Never tied to enforcement decisions

Why This Matters

Discovery should be earned, not gamed. SS Clips exists to rebuild trust between platforms and creators by making the rules visible — and enforcing them consistently.
