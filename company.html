Founder

Founder & CEO

Deondre

Deondre is the owner and founder of the technology company StudentStream and the architect behind its expanding ecosystem of platforms and divisions.

He launched StudentStream to solve long-standing problems in creator monetization, platform control, and digital ownership that affect creators across every industry.

What began as a single idea has evolved into a multi-division company focused on streaming, creator tools, production, legal infrastructure, and next-generation digital services.

“We’re not just building platforms. We’re building infrastructure for the creator economy.”

Divisions

StudentStream Studios

A creative and production division supporting digital creators, filmmakers, and media projects through professional tools, infrastructure, and development resources.

The core technology platform powering authentication, monetization systems, advertising infrastructure, analytics, and creator services across all divisions.
